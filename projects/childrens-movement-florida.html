Children’s Movement of Florida

My first professional work experience

Looking to get more involved during the second semester of my freshman year at the University of Miami, an opportunity came up when Dave Lawrence Jr. spoke to our "Books that Matter" class. He explained his work promoting early childhood learning, which strongly resonated with me because I had volunteered at the Boys and Girls Club in high school and enjoyed being a role model for kids. I spent the semester helping promote the cause on campus and then interned for the organization over the summer. It was my first real taste of true professionalism, requiring me to show up on time, manage work deliverables, and sit in corporate meetings. One of my main projects involved digging deep into county websites and calling local officials to build an Excel database tracking policy differences across Florida. It is funny looking back on it now because creating that accurate repository took me an entire summer of manual research, whereas these days, Claude could probably pull it together in a matter of minutes.
