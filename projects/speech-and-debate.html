High School Speech and Debate

My favorite high school sport

I started my high school debate career in Lincoln-Douglas debate, transitioned to competing primarily in Public Forum, and finished with Big Questions by my senior year. Along the way, I won several local tournaments and top speaker awards. The only non-regional competition I attended was the summer tournament at the Victory Briefs Institute (VBI) Chicago camp in 2016. There, my partner and I took second place, losing in the finals to Ryan Jiang, who was ranked #2 in the country for Public Forum, despite us beating him earlier in the preliminary rounds. While I never competed outside our local circuit during the regular season, I was incredibly proud to captain our team senior year and provide the coaching I never had access to starting out. My proudest achievement was leading our school to a victory in Congressional Debate. As the most team-oriented format, we hadn't even placed in it when I was a freshman, which truly demonstrated how far our program had come.
