2024–Present Entrepreneurship

Founders Run Club

Stretch; run; talk about entrepreneurship. Eat.

I went to Y Combinator Startup School East in Boston in 2024. Someone in the Slack event said there was a Run Club for founders, and we were all invited. I thought this was such a freaking cool idea. It merged two things I do: try to make startups and be a casual runner. At the time, the only chapters in the U.S. were San Francisco and Boston. I reached out to the global founder after the event and asked if I could start a chapter in Miami. He told me what I had to do, and so I did it. Our club started with just me and a friend going to the run. Over time, it has built into a really solid community of regulars and entrepreneurs in town in Miami stopping by for a run. We have had people start ventures together, start dating each other, become roommates, and become best friends. I am really proud of the community I have created, even if it means committing to getting up early every Saturday morning to run in Miami's hot, sticky weather.
